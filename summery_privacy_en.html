Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how aiSummerizer ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Key Points:
  • All recordings and transcripts are stored locally on your device
  • Audio files are sent to OpenAI for transcription processing
  • We use Firebase Analytics for app usage analytics
  • No user accounts are required or created
  • You can delete all your data at any time

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use aiSummerizer, you may provide:

  • Audio Recordings: Audio and video files you record or import into the App
  • Meeting Titles: Custom names you assign to your recordings
  • Transcripts: AI-generated transcriptions of your audio
  • Summaries: AI-generated summaries of your meetings
  • Chat Messages: Questions you ask the AI about your meeting content

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect certain technical and usage information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Usage Data: App features used, screen views, session duration
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, errors, and app performance metrics
  • Analytics Events: App opens, recordings created, exports performed, AI features used

1.3 Permission-Based Information

The App requests the following device permissions:

Permission Purpose Required?
Microphone To record audio from meetings and conversations Yes, for recording features
Calendar To suggest meeting titles from your calendar events Optional
Speech Recognition For potential on-device transcription capabilities (currently unused) No
File System To save, import, and export audio files and documents Yes, for storage features
Photo Library To import video files containing audio Optional

You can manage these permissions in your device settings at any time. Denying permissions may limit certain App features.

2.1 To Provide and Improve the Service

  • Process your audio files to generate transcriptions
  • Create AI-powered summaries of your meetings
  • Enable AI chat functionality for asking questions about your content
  • Save and organize your recordings locally on your device
  • Suggest meeting titles from your calendar (if permission granted)
  • Allow you to import and export recordings

2.2 Analytics and App Improvement

  • Understand how users interact with the App
  • Identify and fix technical issues and crashes
  • Analyze feature usage to improve the user experience
  • Track app performance and reliability
  • Make informed decisions about new features

2.3 Customer Support

  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Troubleshoot technical issues
  • Provide updates about the App

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any other third parties for their marketing purposes. We do not share your information except as described in this Privacy Policy or with your explicit consent.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All of your recordings, transcripts, and summaries are stored locally on your device in the following ways:

  • Audio Files: Saved in the App's private document directory on your device
  • Meeting Data: Stored in a local SQLite database on your device
  • User Preferences: Saved in device storage (AsyncStorage)

Important: We do not provide cloud backup. If you uninstall the App or reset your device, your data will be permanently deleted unless you have exported it.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data:

  • Data is stored in the App's private sandbox on your device
  • Communications with third-party services use HTTPS encryption
  • We do not store your recordings on our servers
  • Access to device features requires your explicit permission

4.3 Data Retention

  • Local Data: Retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App
  • OpenAI Processing: Subject to OpenAI's data retention policies
  • Analytics Data: Retained by Firebase according to their retention policies (typically 2-14 months)
  • Crash Reports: Retained by Firebase for up to 90 days

5. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control Your Data

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all your recordings and data within the App
  • Export: Export your meetings as text or PDF files
  • Delete: Delete individual recordings or all data through the App settings
  • Modify: Edit meeting titles and other metadata

5.2 Manage Permissions

You can control App permissions through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → aiSummerizer → Permissions
  • Android: Settings → Apps → aiSummerizer → Permissions

5.3 Delete All Data

To completely delete all your data:

  1. Open the App and go to Settings
  2. Tap "Clear All Data"
  3. Confirm deletion

This will permanently delete all recordings, transcripts, and summaries from your device.

5.4 Opt-Out of Analytics

While we don't currently provide an in-app analytics opt-out, you can limit tracking by:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Analytics & Improvements → Disable "Share iPhone Analytics"
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

6. International Data Transfers
