Privacy Policy

SQE Master

Last updated: 22 March 2026

SQE Master (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application (“App”).

SQE Master collects no personal data. All your study data stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

SQE Master does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact details.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.
  • We do not track your location, device identifiers, or browsing activity.

All your study data (flashcards, quiz scores, bookmarks, progress) is stored locally on your device only using on-device storage (SQLite and AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device.

2. No User Accounts

SQE Master does not require you to create an account. There is no registration, login, or user profile. The App is fully anonymous.

3. Subscriptions and Payments

We offer optional subscriptions managed through Apple’s In-App Purchase system:

  • Weekly subscription with a 3-day free trial
  • Annual subscription

Payment and subscription management is handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment details. Apple’s own Privacy Policy governs the information they process for purchases.

4. Third-Party Service: RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat solely to manage subscription status (e.g., whether your subscription is active, expired, or in a free trial). RevenueCat receives only anonymised subscription identifiers provided by Apple. We do not share any personal data with RevenueCat.

RevenueCat’s Privacy Policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or distribute any user data to any third parties, except as described in Section 4 for subscription management.

6. Data Security

Because all study data is stored locally on your device and we do not operate servers that hold user data, the security of your information is managed by your device’s built-in protections (passcode, encryption, etc.).

7. Children’s Privacy

SQE Master is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

8. Your Rights (GDPR & UK GDPR)

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, rectify, erase, or port. If you have concerns about your data rights, please contact us at the address below.

9. Data Retention

All data stored on your device is controlled entirely by you. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the App or using the in-app reset options.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
