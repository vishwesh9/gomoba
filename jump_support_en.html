Frequently Asked Questions

Jump detection isn't working properly. What should I do?

+

If the app isn't detecting your jumps accurately, try these steps:

  • Lighting: Ensure you're in a well-lit area. The AI needs good lighting to detect your body.
  • Full Body Visibility: Make sure your entire body is visible in the camera frame, from head to feet.
  • Camera Position: Position your phone at hip height or slightly lower, about 6-10 feet away.
  • Stable Surface: Place your phone on a stable surface or use a tripod.
  • Calibration: Try recalibrating in Settings > Workout Settings > Recalibrate Detection.

Tip: The app works best when wearing contrasting clothing against your background.

How do I calibrate the detection for my jumping style?

+

Calibration helps the app adapt to your unique jumping style:

  • Go to Settings > Workout Settings
  • Tap Recalibrate Detection
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to perform a few sample jumps
  • The app will adjust sensitivity for your height and jumping pattern

You can recalibrate anytime if you change your workout space or shoes.

How do I restore my Pro subscription?

+

If you previously purchased Pro and it's not showing up:

  • Open Jump Rope Counter
  • Go to Profile > Subscription
  • Tap Restore Purchases
  • Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase

If restore doesn't work, please contact us with your purchase receipt.

How do I cancel my subscription?

+

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store:

  • Open Settings on your iPhone
  • Tap your name at the top
  • Tap Subscriptions
  • Find Jump Rope Counter and tap it
  • Tap Cancel Subscription

You'll retain access to Pro features until the end of your billing period.

Workouts aren't syncing to Apple Health. How do I fix this?

+

To enable Apple Health sync:

  • Open iPhone Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices
  • Find Jump Rope Counter
  • Enable permissions for Active Energy

If already enabled, try toggling off and on again. Also ensure Health integration is enabled in the app under Settings > Health Integration.

How accurate is the calorie calculation?

+

Our calorie calculations are estimates based on:

  • Number of jumps completed
  • Workout duration and intensity
  • Average metabolic equivalents (METs) for jump rope exercise

For more accurate tracking, we recommend using a heart rate monitor paired with Apple Health. Individual factors like weight, fitness level, and jump intensity affect actual calories burned.

Does the app work without internet?

+

Yes! Jump Rope Counter works completely offline:

  • All jump detection happens on your device
  • Workouts are saved locally
  • Statistics and achievements work offline

Internet is only needed for subscription verification and app updates.

Why does the app need camera access?

+

The camera is essential for our AI-powered jump detection:

  • The camera captures your movements in real-time
  • AI analyzes your body position to detect jumps
  • All processing happens on-device - no video is ever uploaded

Your privacy is protected: We never record, store, or transmit any camera footage.
