Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 12, 2025

At Swipe Photo Cleaner, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data.

Key Privacy Principle: Your photos never leave your device. We do not upload, store, or transmit your photos to our servers or any third-party servers. All photo processing happens locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Photos and Media (Local Processing Only)

The App requires access to your device's photo library to provide its core functionality. When you grant photo library permissions:

  • Photo Metadata: We access photo metadata including filename, file size, creation date, modification date, and dimensions
  • EXIF Data: Camera model, location data (if embedded in photos), and other technical information
  • Photo Content: For AI-powered features (blur detection, duplicate detection), we analyze photo content using on-device machine learning
IMPORTANT: All photo processing is performed locally on your device. Your photos are NEVER uploaded to our servers, cloud storage, or shared with third parties. We have no access to your actual photo files.

1.2 Usage Analytics

We collect anonymous usage data through Firebase Analytics to improve the App:

  • App opens, crashes, and error reports
  • Features used within the App (e.g., swipe actions, smart clean usage)
  • Navigation patterns and screen views
  • Session duration and frequency
  • General app performance metrics

This data is anonymized and does not include any personally identifiable information or photo content.

1.3 Device Information

We automatically collect certain device information:

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Operating system version (iOS/Android)
  • App version
  • Unique device identifiers (for analytics purposes only)
  • Language preference
  • Time zone

1.4 Subscription and Purchase Data

When you make in-app purchases:

  • Transaction information is processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store
  • Subscription status is managed through RevenueCat
  • We receive confirmation of purchase and subscription status but do NOT receive your payment card details
  • An anonymous user ID is generated to link your device to your subscription

1.5 Local Storage Data

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Your keep/maybe/trash decisions for photos
  • Bookmarked photos list
  • App preferences (theme, swipe sensitivity, etc.)
  • Progress tracking and statistics
  • Achievement data and streaks
  • 30-day deletion queue

1.6 Communications

If you contact us via email for support or feature requests:

  • Your email address
  • Device and app information you choose to share
  • Content of your messages

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Core App Functionality

  • To provide photo organization and cleanup features
  • To perform on-device AI analysis (blur detection, duplicate detection, categorization)
  • To manage the 30-day deletion queue
  • To save your preferences and app state

2.2 App Improvement

  • To analyze app usage patterns and improve user experience
  • To identify and fix bugs and crashes
  • To develop new features based on user behavior
  • To optimize app performance

2.3 Subscription Management

  • To manage premium subscriptions and verify purchase status
  • To provide access to premium features
  • To process subscription renewals and cancellations

2.4 Customer Support

  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • To troubleshoot technical issues
  • To send important updates about the App (if you've contacted us)

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • Local Processing: Photos are processed entirely on your device, eliminating transmission risks
  • Secure Communication: All network communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Limited Data Collection: We collect only the minimum data necessary for app functionality
  • Anonymous Analytics: Analytics data is anonymized and cannot be traced back to individual users

However, please note that no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Data Retention

5.1 Local Data

  • Data stored locally on your device remains until you delete the App or clear its data
  • Photos in the trash are automatically deleted after 30 days
  • You can manually clear all app data through device settings

5.2 Analytics Data

  • Firebase Analytics retains data according to Google's data retention policies (typically 2-14 months)
  • Crash logs are retained for debugging purposes and automatically deleted after resolution

5.3 Subscription Data

  • RevenueCat retains subscription data for as long as your subscription is active, plus applicable retention periods for financial records
