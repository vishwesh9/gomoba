Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What is the German Citizenship Test app?
The German Citizenship Test app is an educational tool designed to help you prepare for the official German citizenship test (Einbürgerungstest). It includes all 460 official questions in 10 languages, mock tests, lessons, progress tracking, and AI-powered analytics to help you succeed.
Q: What languages are supported?
The app supports 10 languages: German (Deutsch), English, Turkish (Türkçe), Arabic (العربية), Polish (Polski), Ukrainian (Українська), Romanian (Română), Italian (Italiano), Russian (Русский), and Persian (فارسی). You can switch languages anytime in Settings.
Q: Is the content official and up to date?
Yes! The app contains all 460 official questions from the German citizenship test, including questions for all 16 Bundesländer (federal states). We regularly update the content to match current test requirements.
Q: What's the difference between Free and Premium?
Free Version: Access to 50 questions, Chapter 1, 1 mock test per day, basic analytics.

Premium Version: Unlimited access to all 460 questions, all chapters, unlimited mock tests, custom test builder, complete analytics, Bundesland content, and ad-free experience.
Q: How much does Premium cost?
Premium is available as an annual or weekly subscription. Pricing varies by region and is displayed in the app. The annual plan offers the best value with significant savings compared to weekly billing.
Q: Is there a free trial?
Yes! Some subscription plans include a 3-day free trial. If you don't cancel before the trial ends, you'll be charged for the subscription. Free trials are available once per user.
Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
To cancel your subscription:
1. Open iOS Settings
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Select "German Citizenship Test"
5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.
Q: Can I restore my purchase on a new device?
Yes! Tap "Restore Purchases" in the app's subscription/paywall screen. Make sure you're using the same Apple ID that you used for the original purchase.
Q: How do I request a refund?
All purchases are processed through the Apple App Store. To request a refund, contact Apple Support directly or visit reportaproblem.apple.com. Sign in with your Apple ID and select the purchase you'd like to request a refund for.
Q: Does my data sync across devices?
No. The app stores all data locally on your device for privacy. Your progress does not sync to the cloud or across devices. If you delete the app or switch devices, you'll lose your progress unless you restore from an iOS device backup.
Q: How do I delete my data?
Go to Settings → Danger Zone → "Clear All Progress" to delete your study history and progress. To completely remove all data, use "Reset Everything" or uninstall the app.
Q: What is the AI Pass Prediction feature?
The AI Pass Prediction analyzes your test performance, topic mastery, and study patterns to estimate your likelihood of passing the actual citizenship test. It helps you know when you're ready to take the real exam.
Q: Which Bundesland should I select?
Select the German federal state (Bundesland) where you will take the citizenship test. The actual exam includes 3 questions specific to your Bundesland. You can change this selection anytime in Settings.
Q: How many questions do I need to pass the real test?
The official German citizenship test has 33 multiple-choice questions: 30 general questions and 3 Bundesland-specific questions. You need to answer at least 17 correctly to pass (approximately 51% passing score).

Troubleshooting Guide

App Crashes or Freezes

  • Force close and restart: Double-tap home button (or swipe up), swipe away the app, then reopen it
  • Update the app: Check the App Store for available updates
  • Restart your device: Power off and back on
  • Reinstall: Delete and reinstall the app (note: you'll lose progress unless backed up)
Tip: If crashes persist, contact support with your device model and iOS version.

Subscription Issues

Problem: "Restore Purchases" doesn't work

  • Ensure you're connected to the internet
  • Verify you're using the same Apple ID for the original purchase
  • Wait a few minutes after purchase before restoring
  • Restart the app and try again

Problem: Charged but still showing as Free

  • Tap "Restore Purchases" in the app
  • Check your Apple ID subscriptions to verify the purchase went through
  • Wait 5-10 minutes as processing can take time
  • If issue persists after 24 hours, contact support

Problem: Can't cancel subscription

  • Subscriptions are managed through Apple, not in our app
  • Follow the cancellation steps in the FAQ above
  • Contact Apple Support if you can't access subscription settings

Progress Not Saving

  • Ensure you have sufficient storage space on your device
  • Don't force close the app immediately after completing a test
  • Check if "Low Power Mode" is interfering with background saves
  • Try the "Clear Cache" option in Settings (won't delete progress)

Notifications Not Working

  • Check app permissions: iOS Settings → Notifications → German Citizenship Test → Allow Notifications
  • Enable in app: App Settings → Notifications → Toggle on
  • Do Not Disturb: Check if Do Not Disturb mode is enabled
  • Scheduled Focus: Check Focus settings aren't blocking notifications

Language/Translation Issues

  • Change language in: Settings → Language
  • Restart the app after changing language
  • Some minor UI elements may remain in English (interface language)
  • Report any mistranslations to support for fixing

Audio (Text-to-Speech) Not Working

  • Ensure device volume is turned up and not muted
  • Check that "Audio" is enabled in app Settings
  • Download the language voice pack in iOS Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices
  • Some languages may have limited TTS support

Study Tips & Best Practices

How to Use This App Effectively

1. Create a Study Plan

  • Set your test date in the app to create urgency
  • Aim to study 15-30 minutes daily (consistency is key)
  • Set a daily study goal in Settings
  • Enable notifications for study reminders

2. Master Each Chapter

  • Start with Chapter 1: Politics in Democracy
  • Read the lessons before attempting questions
  • Use flashcards for difficult questions
  • Complete all chapters before taking full mock tests

3. Practice Strategically

  • Take regular mock tests to simulate real exam conditions
  • Focus on "Weak Topics" tests to improve problem areas
  • Review incorrect answers immediately to learn from mistakes
  • Use the Custom Test Builder to target specific topics

4. Track Your Progress

  • Check your pass prediction regularly
  • Aim for 80%+ accuracy before taking the real test
  • Review analytics to identify weak areas
  • Maintain a study streak for consistency

5. Select Your Bundesland

  • Choose the correct federal state in Settings
  • Study the 10 Bundesland-specific questions carefully
  • Know the capital city, coat of arms, and basic facts about your state
Pro Tip: Most successful users study for 2-4 weeks and complete at least 10 full mock tests before taking the actual exam.

About the Official Test

Test Format

  • Total Questions: 33 multiple-choice questions
  • General Questions: 30 questions covering all topics
  • Bundesland Questions: 3 questions specific to your federal state
  • Passing Score: 17 correct answers (51%)
  • Time Limit: 60 minutes (you can finish earlier)
  • Format: Paper-based or computer-based (depending on location)

Test Content Areas

  • Politics in Democracy (elections, government, rights)
  • History and Responsibility (Nazi era, division, reunification)
  • People and Society (education, religion, equality)
  • Your Bundesland (state-specific facts)

Eligibility Requirements

To take the citizenship test, you typically need:

  • At least 8 years of legal residence in Germany
  • Valid residence permit
  • German language proficiency (B1 level)
  • Financial self-sufficiency
  • No serious criminal record

Note: This app prepares you for the test only. For eligibility questions, contact your local citizenship office (Einbürgerungsbehörde) or immigration advisor.
