Welcome to MTG Card Scanner!

MTG Card Scanner helps you easily scan, organize, and manage your Magic: The Gathering card collection. Here's how to get started:

üì∏ Scanning Cards

How to Scan a Single Card

  1. Tap "Browse & Scan" on the home screen
  2. Tap the camera icon
  3. Choose "Take Photo" or "Choose from Library"
  4. Position the card clearly in the frame
  5. The app will automatically recognize the card name
  6. Review the results and add to your collection

üí° Pro Tip: For best results, scan cards in good lighting with minimal glare. Make sure the card name is clearly visible!

Bulk Scanning (Premium Feature)

Premium users can scan multiple cards in one session:

  1. Tap "Bulk Scan" from the Browse screen
  2. Scan cards one after another
  3. Review all scanned cards at once
  4. Add successful scans to your collection in bulk

Scan Troubleshooting

‚ùå Card not recognized?
  • Ensure good lighting without glare or shadows
  • Make sure the card name is fully visible and in focus
  • Try cleaning the card surface if it's dirty or damaged
  • For very old or obscure cards, try manual search instead

üìö Managing Your Collection

Adding Cards to Collection

There are multiple ways to add cards:

  • From Scan Results: After scanning, tap "Add to Collection"
  • From Search: Search for a card and tap the "+" button
  • From Card Details: View any card and tap "Add to Collection"

Card Details You Can Track

  • Quantity: How many copies you own
  • Condition: Mint, Near Mint, Lightly Played, Moderately Played, Heavily Played, or Damaged
  • Foil Status: Regular or foil version
  • Purchase Price: What you paid for the card
  • Notes: Personal annotations or reminders
  • Acquisition Date: When you got the card

Viewing Collection Statistics

Tap the stats icon (üìä) in your Collection tab to see:

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my data backed up to the cloud?

A: Currently, all data is stored locally on your device. We recommend exporting your collection regularly (Premium feature) as a backup. Cloud sync may be added in future updates.

Q: Where do card prices come from?

A: Card prices are sourced from the Scryfall API, which aggregates data from various marketplaces. Prices are for informational purposes only and update periodically.

Q: Can I use the app offline?

A: You can browse your collection and view previously cached cards offline. However, scanning, searching, and price updates require an internet connection.

Q: What happens if I uninstall the app?

A: All locally stored data (collection, decks, preferences) will be permanently deleted. Make sure to export your collection before uninstalling!

Q: When does my scan limit reset?

A: Free users get 10 scans per month. The limit resets on the 1st day of each month at midnight UTC.

Q: Can I scan cards in languages other than English?

A: Card recognition works best with English cards. Other languages may have limited accuracy depending on the card and language.

Q: Why is my collection value different from expected?

A: Collection value is calculated using current market prices from Scryfall. Prices fluctuate daily based on supply, demand, and market conditions. The value shown is an estimate and may not reflect actual selling prices.

Q: How do I request a refund?

A: All purchases are processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Refund requests must be made directly through the platform:

  • iOS: Apple Support
  • Android: Google Play Help

üõ†Ô∏è Troubleshooting

App Crashes or Freezes

  1. Force quit the app and reopen it
  2. Ensure you have the latest version from the App Store/Play Store
  3. Restart your device
  4. If the issue persists, contact support

Scan Not Working

  • Check camera permissions in device settings
  • Ensure good lighting and card is in focus
  • Verify you have an internet connection (OCR requires internet)
  • Check if you've reached your monthly scan limit (free users)

Premium Features Not Working After Purchase

  1. Go to Profile ‚Üí "Restore Purchases"
  2. Close and reopen the app
  3. Check your subscription status in App Store/Play Store
  4. If still not working, contact support with your purchase receipt

Collection Not Showing Correct Stats

  • Pull down to refresh the collection screen
  • Check if any cards have quantity set to 0
  • If issues persist, try clearing cache: Profile ‚Üí Debug Tools ‚Üí Clear Cache

Price Data Not Loading

  • Ensure you have an active internet connection
  • Price data may be temporarily unavailable due to API issues
  • Try again in a few minutes
  • Note: Price visibility is limited for free users
