Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

Your Privacy Matters
German Citizenship Test is committed to protecting your privacy. This app stores all your study data locally on your device and does not sync to the cloud or require an account.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use the German Citizenship Test app, we may collect the following information that you voluntarily provide:

  • User Preferences: Language selection, theme preference (dark/light mode), study goals, and preferred study times
  • Test Date: Your planned German citizenship test date (optional)
  • Federal State Selection: Your chosen Bundesland for state-specific questions
  • Notification Settings: Your preferences for study reminders

Important: All this information is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers or the cloud.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

The app automatically collects the following information to provide and improve functionality:

Study Progress Data (Local Storage Only)

  • Test scores and results (pass/fail, time taken, questions answered)
  • Lesson completion status and reading progress
  • Bookmarked questions and incorrectly answered questions
  • Topic-wise performance scores and weak area identification
  • Study time tracking (daily and total minutes studied)
  • Experience points (XP), level, and achievement unlocks
  • Daily study streaks (current streak, longest streak)

Storage: All progress data is stored locally in an encrypted SQLite database on your device.

Anonymous Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app and improve the experience. Analytics data collected includes:

  • App open events and screen views
  • Feature usage (test starts/completions, lesson views)
  • Test performance statistics (scores, pass/fail - without personal identification)
  • Subscription events (views, purchases)
  • Device type and operating system version

Important: Analytics are completely anonymous and contain no personally identifiable information (PII). We cannot identify you individually from analytics data.

Crash Reports

We use Firebase Crashlytics to identify and fix app crashes. Crash reports may include:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version and crash timestamps
  • Technical crash logs and stack traces

Crash reports do not contain personal information or your study data.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Local Data Usage

Data stored locally on your device is used to:

  • Save your study progress and test results
  • Provide personalized recommendations for weak areas
  • Track your learning streak and achievements
  • Maintain your preferences and settings
  • Schedule study reminder notifications (if enabled)

2.2 Analytics Usage

Anonymous analytics data is used to:

  • Understand how users interact with the app
  • Identify popular features and areas for improvement
  • Monitor app performance and stability
  • Improve the overall user experience

2.3 Subscription Management

When you make an in-app purchase, transaction data is processed by:

  • Apple App Store: Processes all payments and stores payment information
  • RevenueCat: Manages subscription status and entitlements using an anonymous user ID

We do not have access to your payment card details. All payment processing is handled securely by Apple.

3.2 No Data Selling

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties for marketing purposes. We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process, or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your personal data and study progress is stored locally on your device using:

  • SQLite Database: Encrypted local database for questions, progress, and test results
  • Secure Storage: iOS secure storage mechanisms (Keychain) for sensitive settings

Your data never leaves your device except for anonymous analytics and subscription verification.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • iOS platform encryption for all local data
  • HTTPS encryption for all network communications
  • Secure token-based authentication with RevenueCat
  • Regular security updates and patches

4.3 No Cloud Sync

The app does not sync your data to the cloud or across devices. If you delete the app or switch devices, your study progress will be lost unless you restore from an iOS device backup.

5. Data Retention

5.1 Local Data

Data stored on your device is retained indefinitely until you:

  • Delete specific data (e.g., clear test history)
  • Use "Reset Everything" in app settings
  • Uninstall the app

5.2 Analytics Data

Anonymous analytics data is retained according to Firebase's data retention policies:

  • Raw event data: 2 months
  • Aggregated reports: 14 months

5.3 Subscription Data

Purchase and subscription records are retained by Apple and RevenueCat according to their respective policies for accounting, tax, and fraud prevention purposes.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control Your Data

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • View Your Data: All your data is accessible within the app (Settings, Analytics, Test History)
  • Delete Your Data: Use "Clear All Progress" or "Reset Everything" in Settings to delete local data
  • Modify Settings: Change preferences, language, and notifications at any time in Settings

6.2 Notifications

You can control study reminder notifications:

  • Enable/disable notifications in app Settings
  • Change notification times and frequency
  • Revoke notification permission in iOS Settings

6.3 Subscription Management

To manage or cancel your subscription:

  1. Open iOS Settings
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Select "German Citizenship Test"
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

6.4 Data Portability

Currently, the app does not provide automated data export. If you need a copy of your data, contact us at golukicoding@gmail.com and we will assist you.

7. Children's Privacy

The German Citizenship Test app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at golukicoding@gmail.com.

The app is designed for individuals preparing for the German citizenship test, which typically requires applicants to be at least 16 years of age.
