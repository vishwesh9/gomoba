Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my Premium subscription?

Go to the Paywall screen and tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom. This will restore your subscription if you previously purchased on the same Apple ID. Make sure you're signed in with the correct Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the Apple App Store. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions on your iPhone, find "Australian Citizenship Test" and tap "Cancel Subscription". You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

Is the content up to date with the official test?

We regularly update our questions and content to align with the official "Our Common Bond" resource book and Department of Home Affairs guidelines. However, we recommend also reviewing the official materials as the test may include new questions.

What are the passing requirements?

To pass the Australian citizenship test, you must score at least 75% (15 out of 20 questions correct) AND answer ALL 5 Australian Values questions correctly. If you fail any values question, you will not pass even if your overall score is 75% or higher.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! All study content and practice tests work offline once downloaded. Your progress is saved locally on your device.

How many tests can I take per day?

Free users can take 1 mock test per day. Premium users have unlimited access to all tests and features.

What's included in Premium?

Premium includes unlimited mock tests, full access to all learning chapters (500+ questions), detailed analytics to track your progress, Australian Values tracking, and the custom test builder feature.

My progress isn't syncing. What should I do?

Your progress is stored locally on your device. If you reinstall the app or switch devices, you may need to set up your progress again. We're working on cloud sync features for a future update.

I found an error in a question. How do I report it?

We appreciate your help in improving our content! Please email us at golukicoding@gmail.com with the question details, and we'll review and correct it promptly.

Is this app affiliated with the Australian government?

No, this is an independent educational app. We are not affiliated with the Department of Home Affairs or any government body. Our content is based on publicly available information from the official "Our Common Bond" resource book.
