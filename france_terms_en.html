What the App is

The App is a study and practice tool for France’s civic exam (examen civique), required since 1 January 2026 for naturalisation and certain residence permits. It offers structured lessons across the five official subjects, practice questions with explanations, realistic mock exams in the official format (40 multiple-choice questions — 28 knowledge and 12 situational — in 45 minutes, pass mark 32/40), flashcards, and progress tracking. The App works offline and requires no account.
