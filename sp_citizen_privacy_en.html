Introduction

Welcome to US Citizenship Test Prep ("we," "our," or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Our app stores the following information locally on your device using SQLite database:

  • Study Progress: Your answers, quiz results, chapters completed, and performance statistics
  • User Preferences: Language selection (English/Spanish), dark mode settings, and other app preferences
  • Bookmarks: Questions you've marked for review
  • Practice History: Your practice session history and mock exam results

Important: This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or process:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Location data
  • Photos or camera access
  • Contacts
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII)

Analytics Information

We may use Firebase Analytics (or similar anonymous analytics tools) to understand how users interact with the app. This includes anonymous data such as:

  • App opens and session duration
  • Feature usage (which chapters are most studied, which modes are used)
  • Device type and OS version (for compatibility testing)
  • Crash reports (to fix bugs and improve stability)

This analytics data is completely anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally. We use this information solely to improve the app experience.

How We Use Your Information

The information stored locally on your device is used to:

  • Track your study progress and provide personalized recommendations
  • Save your preferences and settings
  • Show you your performance statistics and improvement over time
  • Allow you to resume your studies where you left off

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT share, sell, or rent your data to any third parties.

The only third-party service we may use is Firebase Analytics for anonymous usage statistics, as described above.

Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device using industry-standard SQLite database encryption. We do not transmit your personal study data over the internet. The app works completely offline.

Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally on your device and is accessible to you through the app
  • Deletion: You can delete your progress data at any time through the app settings or by uninstalling the app
  • Control: You can disable analytics in your device settings if you prefer not to send anonymous usage data

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The U.S. citizenship test is designed for adults and individuals age 18 or older who are applying for naturalization.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

International Users

This app is designed for individuals preparing for the U.S. citizenship test. If you are located outside the United States, please note that your data is stored locally on your device and is not transferred internationally.
